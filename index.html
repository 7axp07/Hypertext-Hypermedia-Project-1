What is Mystic Messneger?

Mystic Messenger (KR:수상한메신저) is an otome game developed and published by the South Korean based game company Cheritz Co., Ltd in mid-2016 for Android and iOS. It's a storytelling game in the form of a messenger app, which you, the player, stumble upon accidentally.
